This app brings your Bestway Lay-Z-Spa hot tub into Homey. Once connected with your Bestway account, you can turn the spa on and off, set the target water temperature, and read the current temperature — all from a single place. The AirJet massage intensity can be set to off, low or high, the HydroJet massage can be toggled on and off, and the filter pump can be controlled independently. All functions are available as Flow cards, so you can automate your spa however you like.

To get started, add the device through the Homey app, enter your Bestway Smart Hub login credentials and select your spa from the list. The app will keep the status up to date automatically.

Tested with the Hydrojet SPA Heater with WIFI as used in the Bestway® LAY-Z-SPA® Dominica HydroJet™ Energy Plus whirlpool with app control.
