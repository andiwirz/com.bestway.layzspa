Bring your Bestway Lay-Z-Spa hot tub into Homey and control it from anywhere. Set the water temperature, run the massage jets and manage the filter — all through Flows or the Homey app.

Two connection methods are supported: the classic Bestway Smart Hub account (V01 models) and the newer SmartHub Connect share code (V02 models — Airjet V02, Hydrojet V02 and Ultrafit). To get started, add the device through the Homey app and follow the on-screen instructions for your model.
